Welcome to Pixee AI. Pixee AI is an AI-powered photo editor and art generator that helps users create, enhance, and transform images with AI tools. This page explains how Pixee AI handles data, generated content, and the responsibilities of users and the developer.

3. User Accounts

Pixee AI does not require user registration, login, or personal identification. All features are available without creating an account.

4. Data Collection & Storage

Uploaded images: Images you upload are processed on our servers to perform the requested AI task. Uploaded and generated images are automatically deleted within 1 hour of processing completion.

Non-personal data: We may collect limited non-identifiable data to operate and improve the app, such as:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version and usage logs
  • Anonymous crash reports and analytics

Advertising data: Pixee AI displays ads using Google AdMob. Ad networks may collect anonymized identifiers (e.g., device IDs) to serve relevant ads. For details, see Google's ad policies and privacy documentation.

5. AI Content Disclaimer

Pixee AI uses AI models to generate image results. These outputs are model predictions and may not always match user input or expectations. We do not guarantee visual accuracy or specific outcomes. Users may regenerate or edit results if unsatisfied.

7. Children's Privacy

Pixee AI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover that we have inadvertently collected personal data from a child under 13, we will promptly delete that data.

8. Data Retention
